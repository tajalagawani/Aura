------------------------
1. Check service status:
   systemctl status aura-dashboard

2. Check firewall:
   ufw status
   ufw allow 8080/tcp

3. Check port is listening:
   netstat -tulpn | grep 8080

4. Check logs:
   journalctl -u aura-dashboard -n 50

   systemctl restart aura-monitor
